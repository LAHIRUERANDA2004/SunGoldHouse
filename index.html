Our Legacy

Proudly Serving Since 1994

NEW SUN GOLD HOUSE has been a symbol of trust and excellence in Sri Lankan jewelry for three decades. Our journey began in the heart of Pilimathalawa, where we established our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Each piece in our collection is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest gold and precious stones. Our artisans combine traditional techniques with contemporary designs to create timeless jewelry that celebrates life's special moments.
